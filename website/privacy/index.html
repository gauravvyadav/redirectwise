Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2025

Introduction

RedirectWise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles information when you use RedirectWise.

Information We Collect

RedirectWise is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and process the following information locally on your device only:

  • URLs that you visit and their redirect chains
  • HTTP response headers and status codes
  • Timing information for redirects
  • Your extension preferences and settings

Important: All data is stored locally in your browser using browser storage APIs. We do not transmit, collect, or store any of your browsing data on external servers.

How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used exclusively to:

  • Display redirect chains and HTTP information in the extension popup
  • Calculate SEO scores for redirect chains
  • Maintain a history of analyzed URLs for your reference
  • Generate PDF reports when you request them
  • Remember your preferences (like dark mode settings)

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using browser storage APIs. You can clear this data at any time by:

  • Using the clear history feature within the extension
  • Removing the extension from your browser
  • Clearing your browser's extension data

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties. Since all data remains on your device, there is no data transmission to external servers.

Permissions

RedirectWise requires the following browser permissions to function:

  • webRequest: To monitor HTTP requests and capture redirect information
  • webNavigation: To track page navigation and detect redirects
  • tabs: To associate redirect data with the correct browser tab
  • storage: To save your history and preferences locally

Third-Party Services

RedirectWise does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The extension operates entirely offline and does not make any external network requests.

Children's Privacy

RedirectWise is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
